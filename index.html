ABOUT US

25+ years in the business

KC Medical was founded in 1995 and is a professional ALS & BLS provider for venues and large events in the midwest.

WHY CHOOSE US?

We're certified, experienced, and insured.

KC Medical, Inc. is a Kansas City based EMS company serving since 1995 with coverages of 1500 events per year.

We are the Industry Choice and leaders for on-site medical services. While the majority of our specialized on-site medical services are in the Kansas City Metro Area, we do have a professional, positive presence in the rest of the country along with some International clients and events.


While every event is different in size and nature, we will discuss, design and prepare a top notch on-site medical plan for coverage for your event, while we focus on your budget and the overall health and safety of our patients as will maintain and monitor the impact on the local 9-1-1 system.
